Lasagna

Desription

This is a vegeterian lasagna dish that is to die for! Tons of flavor and I prefer this over a meaty lasagna any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 32 oz can of tomato sauce
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 2 eggplants
  • diced olives
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup mozarella cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • 8 oz cottage cheese

Steps

  1. Cook veggies and drain water
  2. Make tomato sauce
    1. Heat garlic in olive oil until fragrant
    2. Add can of crushed tomatoes
    3. Salt to taste
    4. Remove from heat and add basil
  3. Make cream sauce
    1. Combine garlic cloves with heavy cream, cottage cheese, salt, pepper, and parmesan cheese. Stir until smooth
  4. Lay down lasagna sheets 1 layer at a time
    1. After each sheet pour equal amount of tomato sauce, cream sauce, cooked veggies, and mozarella cheese
    2. on top layer add a copious amount of mozarella cheese
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 40 minutes until cheese is browned on top
