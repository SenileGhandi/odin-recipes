Green Detox Smoothie

Desription

This drink gonna make you poop out your insides that you've abused drinking like a fish over the holidays. Don't worry it's only green going down, brown going out!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/4 pear, chopped
  • 1/4 avocado, chopped
  • brocolli florets

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients together until homogenous and smooth!
  2. Drink up you dummie!
